Industrial :

Our industrial model process ensures that we can produce industrial models, quickly and cost effectively by our team of model makers and engineers utilizing comprehensive CNC and rapid prototyping facilities. We are often asked to make industrial models that are representations of industrial machines and equipment, this could be a simplistic concept of what the end result might look like, or it could be a fully functioning model.
