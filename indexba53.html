Architectural

Architecture :

An architectural model is a type of a scale model, representing of a structure built to study aspects of an architectural design and to communicate design ideas to clients, committees, and the general public. Architectural models are a tool which may be used for show, presentation, obtaining permits, and sale purposes. Models can be made quickly using cardboard, wooden blocks, acrylic sheets, mount boards , foam boards and other materials.
